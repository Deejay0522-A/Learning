## Dahlia
Race and Traits: Vampire
\
Age: 23 (1961)
\
Birthday: 12/05/1938
\
Gender: Female

Occupation: Unemployed (Ex Lab Rat)


Personality: 
\
Often cold, tends to keep to themselves and doesn't go out of their way to interact with others. Warms up quickly to others that interact first. 

Life Philosophy:
\
"I dont care; unless it involves me."


Appearance:
\
With an alluring and slender figure, she often wears black and clothes fully due to dispositioning from her hometown in Ishim, Russia. 

Backstory:
\
Born in Ishim, Russia, she lived a normal life until she voluenteered for a Soviet Lab program. During this program, she was given the stone mask, in which she became a vampire. After this, they kept testing her in Soviet research, finding new ways to improve their army and combat NATO. After months of containment, she then was given the Arrow which was administered via an injection with the metal scraps of the arrow. After the shot was administered, Dahlia suffered a fatal-looking seizure, being pronounced dead and thrown into the snowy woods of northern Russia to feed the wolves. After a while, she came to, at a campfire with a strange lady, her stand.
